What is Upland?
Upland is a virtual world built on blockchain technology, where players can buy, sell, and trade virtual properties based on real-world addresses. Players can earn rewards, participate in events, and grow their portfolios as they build their own virtual empire. Upland combines the excitement of real estate with the power of blockchain, offering a unique gaming experience with real-world value.
How do I earn money in Upland?
There are many ways for players to earn in Upland. Players can flip properties, create and sell digital assets in metaventures (player-owned and operated shops), or win competitions. They can earn either the in-app currency UPX or sell their digital assets for USD. This is made possible through Upland's digital NFT-to-USD program, in partnership with Tilia Pay. The program allows players to list digital assets directly for US dollars, so when another player makes a purchase, the payment is deposited directly into the seller's account.
What is $SPARKLET?
Upland's Sparklet ($SPARKLET) is the utility token of the Upland web3 gaming platform, designed to power its digital economy and enhance user experiences. It plays a critical role in building, creating, and maintaining assets within the Upland ecosystem. Key features and uses of $SPARKLET:
  • World Building: Players can stake $SPARKLET to construct digital structures tied to virtual properties on the Upland map
  • User-Generated Content (UGC): It facilitates the creation of blockchain-minted map assets through no-code minting processes
  • Energy Source: $SPARKLET powers in-game mechanics like transportation and racing
  • Renting and Staking: Holders can rent out $SPARKLET for UPX (Upland's in-game currency) and stake it in layer 2 applications
  • Bridging: Designed for Upland's infrastructure, $SPARKLET can be seamlessly bridged between Upland and Ethereum (ERC-20 token)
$SPARKLET is available on selected centralized and decentralized exchanges. For the latest price information, see Coinmarketcap. Get $SPARKLET here.
How do I join Upland?
Upland can be accessed on mobile devices or from browsers. To start collecting digital properties, all you need is an email address. That’s it! There’s no need to set up a crypto wallet. Upland’s innovative onboarding system automatically creates a blockchain account for you once you’ve acquired enough assets in the game and become a full-fledged Uplander.
Alternatively, you could explore as a guest without setting up an account. However, the experience is limited to looking around the map. Setting up an account takes minutes, and then you can jump into the fun!
